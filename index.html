◈ About Zentrox

The Future of
Content Freedom

We built Zentrox because downloading Instagram content shouldn't require a PhD. It should be instant, clean, and free — for everyone.

🛰

Our Mission

Zentrox exists to give every creator and consumer instant access to their own content. Instagram doesn't make it easy to save your memories — we do.

🧬

Our Tech

We use a lightweight Node.js/Express backend with intelligent URL parsing to detect and fetch content securely with zero data retention.

🌍

Our Promise

Free forever. No ads. No tracking. No email required. We believe digital freedom is a right, not a premium feature.

The Story

Zentrox started as a side project in 2024 — a simple tool for a creator who kept losing access to their own Reels after account changes. That frustration became fuel for something bigger.

Today, Zentrox serves millions of users across 180 countries, powered by a lean architecture that prioritizes speed, privacy, and simplicity above all else.

We're not a massive corporation. We're a small, passionate team that believes the best tools get out of your way and just work.

◈ Privacy Policy

Your Privacy
Is Sacred

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Zentrox is designed with a zero-data philosophy. We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. When you submit an Instagram URL, it is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after serving the download link. No logs are retained.

2. Cookies

We use no tracking cookies. We may use a single session cookie to maintain basic functionality within your browser session. This cookie expires when you close your browser and contains no personally identifiable information.

3. Third-Party Services

Zentrox fetches media directly from Instagram's public CDN on your behalf. We do not share your URL or request data with any third parties beyond what is technically necessary to retrieve the content you requested.

4. Analytics

We use anonymous, aggregated analytics (total download counts, error rates) to improve the service. These metrics contain no individual identifiers and cannot be linked back to you.

5. Children's Privacy

Zentrox is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Zentrox after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Major changes will be announced on our website.

7. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, contact us at privacy@zentrox.app.
